About Me

 Kangzheng Liu is currently a dual Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science and Technology of the Huazhong University of Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. Feng Zhao, and the School of Computer Science of the University of Technology Sydney, supervised by Prof. Guandong Xu. Prior to that, Kangzheng obtained his B.Sc. in Computer Science and Technology from Hefei University of Technology in 2021.

Kangzheng's research scopes include but are not limited to Knowledge Graphs, Graph Learning, Multimodal. Kangzheng is also interested in other research areas of graph-related application such as Cognition-Driven Modeling and GNN-LLMs.
